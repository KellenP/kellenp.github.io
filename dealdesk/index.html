Underwriting is the product

The numbers your buyer can't re-trade at inspection.

In a softer Austin market, deals don't die at contract — they die at the inspection, when the rehab number was wrong. Every deal on this desk ships with the repair scope and the comps that justify it, so a buyer commits with confidence and closes on time.

  • ①
    Real comps, not guesses. ARV backed by four recent, like-kind sales in the same submarket.
  • ②
    Scoped rehab. A line-item repair budget so the flip math holds when the inspector shows up.
  • ③
    Both exits modeled. Flip profit and rental cap rate, because your buyers run different playbooks.
